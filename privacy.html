Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

RailmountGame.com ("we", "us", "this site") is a fan site dedicated to the train puzzle game Railmount by Hempuli. This page explains what limited information we collect, how it is used, and the third-party services we rely on.

Information We Collect

RailmountGame.com does not require an account, does not host forms that collect personal information, and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable data. We do not store user profiles, email addresses, or payment information on this site.

Cookies and Local Storage

This site itself does not set tracking cookies. However, the third-party services we embed (see below) may set their own cookies or use local storage for their own functionality. You can disable cookies at the browser level at any time without affecting your ability to read our content.

Third-Party Services

  • itch.io embed. The Railmount game is embedded from itch.io via an iframe. itch.io may set cookies and collect usage data according to its own privacy policy. Please review itch.io's policy at itch.io/docs/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Google Fonts. We load the Outfit typeface from Google Fonts. Google receives a request for the font file containing your IP address; no cookies are set.
  • Google AdSense (planned). We may display ads delivered by Google AdSense, which uses cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com.
  • Analytics (planned). We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate traffic. No personally identifiable information is tied to any analytics events.

Children's Privacy

Railmount is a family-friendly puzzle game and the content on this site is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data directly, there is typically nothing to request, update, or delete on our end. If you believe a third-party service embedded on our site has collected data about you, please contact that service directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect new services or legal requirements. When we do, the "last updated" date at the top of this page will change. For questions, please visit our contact page.
