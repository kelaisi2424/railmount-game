What is Railmount?

Railmount is an inventive train puzzle game that challenges you to lay down tracks and guide locomotives to their destinations. In each level, you are presented with a grid where trains need to travel from point A to point B, and it is up to you to draw the connecting rails. The Railmount game combines spatial thinking with creative route planning, making every puzzle a satisfying challenge to solve. You can play Railmount online right here in your browser with zero setup required.

What sets Railmount apart from other browser puzzle games is the elegant design philosophy of its creator. Hempuli, the indie developer behind the critically acclaimed Baba Is You, brings the same knack for clever rule-based mechanics to this train puzzle game. Each level introduces new constraints and ideas that keep the experience fresh. Whether you are a longtime fan of Hempuli games or discovering this developer for the first time, Railmount online delivers a polished and thought-provoking puzzle experience you can enjoy anywhere.

How to Play Railmount

  1. 1 Observe the level layout — identify where each train starts and where it needs to arrive.
  2. 2 Use your mouse (or finger on mobile) to drag and draw rail segments connecting the routes.
  3. 3 Make sure every train can safely reach its destination without collisions or dead ends.
  4. 4 Complete the puzzle to unlock progressively more complex and rewarding challenges.
