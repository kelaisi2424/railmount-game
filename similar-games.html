10 Best Games Like Railmount (2026)

Finished Railmount and craving more? These ten train, logic, and route-planning puzzle games scratch the same itch as Hempuli's beloved rail-drawer.

Advertisement

Why These Games Made the List

Railmount sits at a very specific intersection: it is a browser-friendly puzzle game that combines hand-drawn track-laying with tight, rule-based design. Finding other games with the same vibe is not easy, but the ten below come the closest. Some share the literal train mechanic, others share Hempuli's minimalist rule-bending philosophy, and a handful share both. Whether you want more train puzzle games or just more brain-bending logic in the same style, this list has something for you.

If you have not yet explored our deeper guides, start with our Railmount walkthrough or our Railmount tips page before moving on. Ready to discover what to play next? Let us dig in.

01.

Baba Is You

Developer: Hempuli · Logic puzzle

The obvious starting point. Baba Is You is Hempuli's breakout puzzle hit, where you physically push around the game's rules to change what wins, what stops you, and what you even are. If Railmount's clean design charmed you, this is the deeper cave.

Why it is similar: Same developer, same belief that puzzle design should teach through play rather than tutorials. Every level is a miniature logic lesson.

Where to play: Steam, Nintendo Switch, iOS, Android. A free demo is available on Hempuli's itch.io.

02.

Cosmic Express

Developer: Draknek & Friends · Train route puzzle

The closest mechanical match for Railmount. You draw train routes through alien spaceports, picking up colored passengers and dropping them at matching stations. The joy is in the clever layouts that force counter-intuitive loops.

Why it is similar: Identical rail-drawing input, matching "plan then run" gameplay loop, and a similarly relaxed visual style.

Where to play: Steam, iOS, Android.

03.

Train Valley

Developer: Flazm · Train strategy

A warmer, more dynamic take on rail building. You lay track across historical periods, route real-time trains between stations, and juggle switches to avoid crashes. Less puzzle, more puzzle-flavored strategy.

Why it is similar: Train routing, junction timing, and a strong emphasis on planning under pressure.

Where to play: Steam (original) and Steam/mobile for the sequel Train Valley 2.

04.

Mini Motorways

Developer: Dinosaur Polo Club · Route-planning puzzle

Mini Motorways swaps rails for roads, but the core mental gymnastics — draw the path, keep traffic flowing, adapt as complexity rises — feel instantly familiar. Beautiful minimalist art seals the deal.

Why it is similar: Same drag-to-draw connection input, same zen-until-it-isn't pacing.

Where to play: Steam, Apple Arcade, Nintendo Switch.

05.

Railbound

Developer: Afterburn · Puzzle

Railbound has you placing rail tiles to hook up cargo trains in the correct order. It is gentler than Railmount but shares the "one more puzzle" rhythm of Hempuli's design, wrapped in cozy cartoon art.

Why it is similar: Tile-based rail puzzles with order-sensitive stations, perfect for a Railmount fan wanting something softer.

Where to play: Steam, iOS, Android, Nintendo Switch.

06.

A Good Snowman Is Hard to Build

Developer: Draknek, Alan Hazelden, Benjamin Davis · Puzzle

Roll snowballs through tight gardens to stack three-tiered snowmen. Its puzzles look adorable but hide the same rigorous spatial thinking Railmount rewards. A perfect palate cleanser between Hempuli sessions.

Why it is similar: Clean grid puzzles, compact levels, and "oh, that is how it works" moments on every third puzzle.

Where to play: Steam, iOS.

07.

Pipe Push Paradise

Developer: Corey Martin · Puzzle

A tiny tropical island full of plumbing puzzles. Push pipes into place to connect water sources, with each puzzle reusing just a handful of tile types in surprising combinations. The economy of ideas feels very Railmount.

Why it is similar: Connection-focused puzzles with minimal UI and maximum "aha".

Where to play: Steam, Nintendo Switch.

08.

Snakebird

Developer: Noumenon Games · Puzzle

Cult classic. You guide elongated bird-snakes through gravity-bound fruit puzzles. The difficulty curve is notorious and the solutions feel like magic when they click — the exact feeling that pulls you through Railmount's late game.

Why it is similar: Pure logic puzzle, grid-based, and a master class in teaching mechanics without tutorials.

Where to play: Steam, iOS, Android, Nintendo Switch.

09.

Patrick's Parabox

Developer: Patrick Traynor · Puzzle

Boxes containing boxes containing boxes. Push blocks around grids where each block can be a whole new grid. The recursive concept is wild, but the puzzle grammar — clean shapes on small grids — is pure Railmount DNA.

Why it is similar: Minimalist visuals, grid-based puzzles, and a relentless escalation of a single elegant idea.

Where to play: Steam.

10.

Recursed

Developer: Portponky · Puzzle platformer

A 2D puzzle platformer where jars are portals into entire sub-levels. Rooms can be moved and re-entered, creating head-spinning recursion puzzles. If you loved the "wait, how did this work?" moments of Railmount, Recursed turns them up to eleven.

Why it is similar: Rule-based puzzle design, compact rooms, and a quiet confidence that you will figure it out.

Where to play: Steam, itch.io.

Similar Games FAQ

What are the best games like Railmount?

The best games like Railmount are Baba Is You (by the same developer), Cosmic Express, Train Valley, Mini Motorways, and Railbound. They all share rail-drawing or clever spatial puzzle mechanics that fans of Hempuli's design will appreciate.

Are there other Hempuli games besides Railmount?

Yes. Hempuli is the studio behind Baba Is You, the award-winning logic puzzle where you change the rules of each level to win. Both games share the same minimalist visuals and rule-based puzzle philosophy that makes Railmount so satisfying.

Can I play any of these games in a browser?

Several of the games on this list are available in browsers via itch.io or have free demos. Others are native downloads on Steam or mobile. Each entry above notes where you can play it so you can pick the format that suits you best.
